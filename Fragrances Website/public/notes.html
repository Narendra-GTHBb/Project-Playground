Fragrance Education

UNDERSTANDING NOTES

Discover how fragrances are structured and experienced.

Fragrances are composed of layers known as notes. Each layer unfolds over time, creating a complete scent experience.

The Notes Pyramid

✶

Top Notes

Light and bright first impressions that sparkle quickly, then fade.

Bergamot • Lemon • Basil

❁

Middle Notes

The heart of the fragrance, balancing freshness with character.

Rose • Jasmine • Cardamom

✦

Base Notes

Deep, long-lasting foundation that remains close to the skin.

Amber • Sandalwood • Musk

Visual Pyramid

A fragrance transitions from an airy opening to a richer, grounded finish.

How It Works

Fragrances evolve over time, transitioning from fresh top notes to deeper base notes.

01

Application

Fresh molecules lift first, giving the initial aura around the skin.

02

Development

The heart appears, revealing floral and spicy facets in balance.

03

Dry Down

Heavier notes remain, leaving a deep signature that lasts for hours.
